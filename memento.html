Memento


2023

When humans first began to use letters, they were carved into stones and animal bones, which naturally weathered after.

Memento gives a pebble-like roundness and quietness to the words that are jotted down or left for someone else. You write, erase, and write again. There is no need to consume paper for each word that you want it to stay there for a while. For the important words that you want to look at slowly, you can leave them there quietly.

The hustle and bustle of the modern world and the flow of primitive time. A product that lies somewhere in between.
