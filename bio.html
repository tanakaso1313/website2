SO TANAKA

ALL FURNITURE LIGHTING OTHER
BIO SHOP

My projects are mainly inspired by personal thoughts or strong interests in materials, philosophy, and other disciplines of art. I believe that the selection of materials, form, and manufacturing process that make up a product are all linguistic in the sense that they are activities by which I perceive the world and share my individual perception of it with others. My motivation is to redefine how I relate to others—society, the environment, and my future self—in a world that is made up of such relationships.
