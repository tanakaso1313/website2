S O T A N A K A

ALL FURNITURE LIGHT OTHER BIO SHOP

My projects are mainly inspired by personal thoughts and strong interests in materials, philosophy, and other artistic disciplines. I believe that the selection of materials, form, and manufacturing processes that comprise a product are all linguistic in nature. They are activities through which I perceive the world and share my individual perception of it with others. My motivation is to redefine how I relate to others, to society, the environment, and my future self, in a world that is fundamentally constituted by such relationships.
